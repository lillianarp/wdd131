Welcome to Tok Stori

What is Tok Stori?

Tok Stori is a journaling and content creation tool.

The phrase, 'tok stori' comes from Melanesian cultures in the Pacific. It refers to telling and sharing stories as a community - creating a shared narrative - in order to make sense of experiences and emotions and to preserve wisdom and learning.

The Tok Stori journaling tool helps us to capture our own daily stories in a way that makes them easy to share.

Capture life's moments, effortlessly

Tok Stori is designed for simplicity and privacy. With just a few clicks, you can record your thoughts, track your experiences, and keep everything securely stored in one place.

Whether you want to jot down a quick note or reflect on a meaningful experience, your entries are always accessible and safe.

Your stories, safely stored

Privacy is built into every feature of Tok Stori. All your entries are securely stored and accessible only to you, ensuring that your reflections stay safe, personal, and always within reach.

From quick notes to meaningful experiences, your stories are kept under lock and key... until you decide what you want to share!

A social kind of journal

Transform your entries into shareable stories with ease. Tok Stori lets you create a polished, 1-minute script from your journal, perfect for a voiceover or quick video.

When you're ready, you can instantly post it to your social media or schedule it for later, all from within the platform.
